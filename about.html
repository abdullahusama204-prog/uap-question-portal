About

Built by students, for students

UAP Question Archive started as a simple idea: past exam papers shouldn't be scattered across group chats and disappearing Google Drive links. This site organizes CT, Mid and Final questions by semester, course and batch, along with a gallery of campus moments, so anyone can find what they need in a few clicks.

Sign in with your UAP email to unlock Previous Questions and the Gallery. You can also bookmark sections and contribute your own question papers and photos — every submission is reviewed by an admin before it goes live.
